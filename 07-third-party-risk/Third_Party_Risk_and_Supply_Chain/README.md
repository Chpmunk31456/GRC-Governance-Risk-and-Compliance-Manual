## Important Notice

This material is provided for education and general third-party risk guidance.

Third-party risk obligations vary according to an organization’s industry, location, contracts, data, systems, regulatory requirements, and dependence on external providers.

Organizations should adapt the guidance to their actual vendor population, risk profile, contractual obligations, and legal or regulatory requirements.
