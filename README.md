# GRC Practical Manuals

## Governance, Risk, Compliance, Resilience, and Security

A free, practical, and beginner-friendly collection of Governance, Risk, and Compliance manuals for students, junior analysts, managers, cybersecurity professionals, auditors, consultants, nonprofit organizations, and teams building or improving security and compliance programs.

**Author:** Alberto (Al) Leiva  
**Repository Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** July 2026

---

## About This Repository

The GRC Practical Manuals collection provides clear and practical guidance for understanding, implementing, documenting, testing, and improving programs related to:

- Cybersecurity governance
- Risk management
- Regulatory compliance
- Privacy
- Assurance and audit readiness
- Cloud security
- Technology risk
- Third-party risk
- Operational resilience

These manuals are designed to help readers move beyond theory and understand how GRC work is performed in real organizations.

The collection includes:

- Plain-language explanations
- Practical implementation guidance
- Framework and regulatory concepts
- Control examples
- Evidence-collection guidance
- Audit-readiness practices
- Risk-assessment methods
- Policy and procedure guidance
- Open-source tools and resources
- Checklists and templates
- Guidance for junior professionals
- Guidance for managers and program leaders

---

## Quick Start

1. Select the framework, regulation, or risk area relevant to your needs.
2. Open the corresponding numbered folder.
3. Open the manual-specific folder.
4. Review the folder’s `README.md`.
5. Download either the Microsoft Word or PDF edition.
6. Adapt the guidance to your organization’s risks, systems, contracts, and obligations.
7. Verify requirements through current official sources.
8. Document implementation and retain appropriate evidence.
9. Test controls regularly.
10. Track gaps, corrective actions, and continuous improvement.
